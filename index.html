Computational Mass Spectrometry, Education Collection

Welcome!

This is a webpage on the upcoming educational collection of articles on Computational Mass Spectrometry.

At the Dagstuhl Seminar on Computational Mass Spectrometry 2013, the organizers and participants decided to create an educational collection of publications on the topic of Computational Mass Spectrometry. The aim of this webpage is to announces this decision to a wider community, coordinate interaction with the community and to collect your feedback.

Organizers

  • Knut Reinert
  • Lennart Martens

If you are interested to contribute to this collection in any way, please contact us using the Feedback Form. Thanks.
