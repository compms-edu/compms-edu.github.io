Welcome!

This is a webpage on the upcoming collection of educational articles on Computational Mass Spectrometry.

At the Dagstuhl Seminar on Computational Mass Spectrometry 2013, the organizers and participants decided that the field is hampered by the lack of proper educational sources. Following on these discussions, we decided to create an educational collection of articles on the topic of Computational Mass Spectrometry which would bridge the gap between texbooks and research articles. This collection could be the source of information for a biologist, chemist, or computational biologist, who would like to learn computational methods of analysis of mass spectrometry data.

The aim of this webpage is to announces this upcoming collection to a wider community at the very early stage, coordinate interaction with the community and help collect your feedback.
